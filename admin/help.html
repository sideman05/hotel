Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and you will receive instructions to reset your password. If you continue to experience issues, please contact support.
How can I generate reports?
Reports can be generated from the Dashboard or specific management sections. Click on the "Reports" tab, select the type of report you need, set the date range and parameters, then click "Generate Report". Reports can be exported in PDF, Excel, or CSV formats.
How do I update room rates for different seasons?
To set seasonal pricing, go to Room Management, select the room you want to update, and click on "Pricing". Here you can set different rates for specific date ranges. You can also bulk update multiple rooms by using the "Seasonal Rates" tool in the Room Management dashboard.
What should I do if a payment fails to process?
First, verify that the payment gateway is properly configured in Settings > Payment. If the settings are correct, check if the guest's card details were entered correctly. You can try processing the payment again or use an alternative payment method. If issues persist, contact our support team with the error details.
How can I customize confirmation emails?
Email templates can be customized in Settings > Email Templates. Select the template you want to modify, make your changes using the editor, and save. You can use placeholders like {guest_name} and {booking_number} to personalize emails with dynamic content.
